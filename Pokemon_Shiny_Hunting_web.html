What are shiny pokemon?

Shiny pokemon are another version of the same pokemon but different color. For example, If you catch a shiny Ponyta, the will get a different colored varient. They are very rare pokemon. They were first introduced in gen 2. (Pokemon Gold, Silver, and Crystal.) But we're only talking about two games Pokemon lets go Pikachu and Eevee.

What are the chances to geting shiny Pokemon?

The chance of getting a shiny pokemon is 1 in 4096. But there is a way to make the chances higher! By doing the catch combo method. This has more chance than the musudo method which cannot be performed in this game. So catch combos are the best thing in this games.

What is a catch combo?

Catch combos are made when you catch a pokemon. if you catch the same pokemon many times your catch combo increases, but there are some things that will break a catch combo.

What will break catch combos?

In this game three things can break catch combos. For instance catching another Pokemon then the pokemon you were comboing will break the combo, closing the game will break the combo, and letting the pokemon of your combo run away from you will break the combo.
