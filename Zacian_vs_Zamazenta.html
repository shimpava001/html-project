Which pokemon are Zacian and Zamazenta?

Zacian is the warrior Pokemon. It is the mascot of the game Pokemon Sword.

Zamazenta is the warrior Pokemon as well. It is the mascot of the game Pokemon Sheild.

They both have to forms. These forms result if they have the item for it. When you catch them they will have the item already.

They also have signature moves. They are Behemoth Blade and Behemoth Bash!

Why Zacian can defeat Zamazenta

When seeing the stats many people might say that Zamazenta can probably beat Zacian. But this is why it cannot.

When you catch Zacian in Pokemon Sword it has close combat which is super effective on Zamazenta. You can probably see where this is going now.
