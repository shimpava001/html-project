How to catch shiny legendary Pokemon in Pokemon lets go

How to find the legendary Pokemon

What do you do when you find the legendary Pokemon?

To start you need to have supplies before you encounter them. What you should have is many berries and many ultra balls. You should also have strong pokemon with super effective moves.

When you finish save in front of the legendary Pokemon and turn of cutscene to make it quick.

The resetting

Encounter the legendary and if it isn't shiny reset and try again. Be patient.

Once it's shiny defeat it with your strong pokemon. If you want to use the master ball you can, but I would save that for Mewtwo.

Happy hunting!
